Introduction

Using Eel & Python , we are able to successfully developed a responsive desktop application that can successfully perform steganography with a cover file and payload. We focus on .docx , img , .mp3 , and .mp4 as the cover file. We focused on the plain text , .docx , img , .mp3 , and .mp4 as the payload.

I was incharge of the .docx to any payload type. I was also tasked to connect up the connectivity of the application , frontend with the logic and file to displaying media .
